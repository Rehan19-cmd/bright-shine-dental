Passionate About Perfect Smiles

Dr. Pooja Bhure-Bansode is a highly qualified and compassionate dental surgeon based in Pune. With over a decade of clinical experience, she specializes in advanced cosmetic dentistry, restorative procedures, and pediatric dental care.

Her philosophy is simple: every patient deserves a healthy, beautiful smile — achieved through gentle care, cutting-edge technology, and personalized treatment plans.

At Bright & Shine Dental Square, Dr. Bansode and her team have created a warm, anxiety-free environment where patients of all ages feel at home. From your very first consultation to your last follow-up, you'll experience care that goes beyond just teeth.
